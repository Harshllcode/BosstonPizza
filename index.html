About Our Pizza

At Bosston Pizza we believe great pizza starts with great ingredients. Our dough rests 48 hours for a light, airy crust. We use San Marzano-style tomatoes, locally-sourced cheeses, and herbs from small farms. Whether you like a classic Margherita or a bold spicy pie, there's a slice waiting.
